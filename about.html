Our Vision

JABS emerges from the vision to transform the housing market into a seamless and accessible experience for young adults, with a special focus on international students who often grapple with daunting challenges when seeking accommodations in bustling cities. Our mission is crystal clear: to redefine the rental journey through the adoption of cutting-edge technology seamlessly blended with a design that's centered around the user’s ease, thus cultivating a platform that elevates the process of finding a home from a task to an experience. At the heart of JABS is the creation of vibrant communities, fostering connections that extend beyond the confines of traditional housing searches. JABS is not merely a tool for finding a place to live; it is the key to unlocking a world where community and a sense of belonging are interwoven into the fabric of the housing experience. Our dedication is to simplify the daunting process of home-finding, transforming it into an enriching journey of social connections and security. JABS directly confronts the economic hurdles and scarcity of suitable living spaces that confront many youths, introducing a pivotal alternative during financially and socially challenging times. Envisioning a service where users are not only able to effortlessly discover but also flourish in their new habitats, JABS stands at the forefront of simplicity, innovation, and affordability in today’s dynamic housing market.

How We're Different

Unlike traditional rental platforms, JABS focuses on community and connection. Our unique 'Bubbles' feature allows like-minded individuals to connect and find shared living spaces, fostering a sense of community in every home.

A Commitment to Continuous Growth

We're constantly improving JABS based on your feedback. Together, we're not just building homes; we're crafting communities, one connection at a time.
